About

CommaQuote is a free, zero-install tool to turn multi-line lists into comma-separated (or any-separator) strings. Paste your data, pick your format, hit Convert, and you're done. No ads, no tracking, no accounts.

FAQ

What separators can I use?

Comma, comma+space, semicolon, pipe, space, tab, or any custom string you type in.

Does this work offline?

Yes — once loaded, the entire tool runs in your browser. No data is sent anywhere.

What's the wrap option?

Wrap wraps the entire output in parentheses, square brackets, or curly braces — handy for SQL IN clauses or JSON arrays.
